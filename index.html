Education Model

1. Every topic starts with definition. This helps student to understand its relevance and relate to usage in life.
2. It is then followed by practice exercises that student does during class sessions. They can do the remaining exercise at home through online portal.
3. Classes are conducted twice a week. Student can come back with any questions, queries from previous sessions and get their doubt clear. Second class in the week is free form and is meant for students to clarify any questions on any topics covered so far.
4. Students are graded per topic and this helps in identifying their strength and areas of improvement.
